Understanding The Notation

The Notation And Symbols Used In Calculus
Most of us take for granted that calculus has a very unique notation when compared to other branches of mathematics. Ignoring the fancy symbols usually allows us to understand the basic arithmetic involved in solving simple calculus problems, but an intuitive understanding of how we arrive at these symbols is important if you wish to take calculus into the real world with you later on. So this section is dedicated to a step by step explanation that will explore the fundamental thinking that has shaped the modern notation as we understand it.
